Section 02  /  Business Case + Proposed Solution

The Problem You're Solving — and How Reeco Closes It.

Three pain points. Three direct answers.

01
The Problem

AP Automation Running on Manual Effort

At a management company operating multiple full-service resorts, invoices arrive daily from dozens of vendors across every property. Without an automated system, each invoice requires manual GL coding, line-item matching, and approval routing — a process that consumes hours of accounting and operations time every week, and leaves room for errors that compound at month-end.

The Answer

Invoices That Code Themselves

Reeco's AP automation uses AI to code every invoice by line item and match it against the corresponding purchase order the moment it arrives. What used to take 7–10 minutes per invoice takes about 20 seconds. The coding model learns over time, getting faster and more accurate with every invoice processed.

02
The Problem

No Real-Time Visibility Across Properties

Without a connected procurement platform, corporate leadership sees spend data only after it has happened — through month-end reports that are already weeks old. GMs order from whatever vendor is convenient, preferred vendor pricing goes unenforced, and price variances aren't caught until the financials close.

The Answer

A Live Checkbook, Not a Lagging Report

Reeco connects every property order to a central dashboard. Every purchase, every vendor, every price — visible in real time from one login. Approval workflows enforce preferred vendor and contract pricing automatically, so variance is addressed before the order ships, not after the month closes.

03
The Problem

Expansion Without a Procurement Playbook

When a management company adds a new property, procurement typically starts from scratch — new vendor relationships, new approval structures, manual onboarding of order guides. There is no system-level framework that a new property can inherit from day one.

The Answer

New Properties Go Live in Days, Not Months

Reeco's onboarding framework puts every new property on your approved vendor catalog, contract pricing, and approval workflows from the first week of operation. Expansion adds a node to a system that is already working — it does not mean starting over.

Section 07  /  FAQs

Questions We Expect — Answered Directly.

If something's missing, email Zander and we'll add it before our first conversation.

"We already have procurement tools in place. Why look at something new?"
The question isn't whether the current setup works in theory — it's whether it works for your team in practice, and whether it's built to scale as your portfolio grows. Reeco is enterprise-grade software purpose-built exclusively for hotels. The difference shows up in day-to-day use: property staff place orders without training sessions, invoices code themselves, and your corporate team sees real spend data without manual intervention.
"How hard is the implementation? We've been through difficult rollouts before."
Reeco does not require a dedicated full-time resource to manage the implementation. We will present a full implementation plan specific to your portfolio at our first meeting — integration sequencing, property rollout phasing, and timeline. Our onboarding team manages the technical configuration end to end.
"What does the integration with our accounting and budget systems look like?"
The specifics depend on your confirmed stack, which we'll review at our first meeting. In general, accounting system integration is configured at kickoff and runs automatically once credentials are set. If you're on Profit Sword, Reeco has a live two-way integration — Reeco pushes daily PO spend, and budget and GL data flows back, giving GMs a real-time checkbook view.
"What about adoption at the property level?"
Reeco is designed for the user who isn't a procurement professional. It looks and behaves like a shopping experience — search for an item, see prices from all your suppliers side by side, submit a purchase order in a few taps. Most property teams are fully self-sufficient within the first week.
"Is our data secure?"
Yes. Reeco is SOC 2 Type II certified. Documentation is available upon request — email zanders@reeco.com and it will be sent within 24 hours.
