Section 02  /  Business Case + Proposed Solution

The Problem You're Solving — and How Reeco Closes It.

Three pain points. Three direct answers.

01
The Problem

Invoices Failing to Reach M3 — Weekly, Without Warning.

Lexima's current system generates incorrect coding, blocked invoices, and duplicates on a weekly basis. When invoices don't flow through to M3, the failure queue has to be manually reviewed — a process that has become a standing part of the team's weekly routine. There's no proactive flagging; the error only surfaces during reconciliation.

The Answer

Invoices Sync to M3 Automatically — No Failure Queue.

Reeco is M3's official Preferred Partner — the integration was built with M3, not around it. GL accounts and vendors sync directly from your M3 instance. Approved invoices flow back automatically, fully coded, with no manual exports and no month-end catch-up. Invoices are visible and viewable from M3 — not just from Reeco.

02
The Problem

Manual Coding That Creates Inconsistency at Scale.

With the current system, GMs code invoices individually with no intelligence that learns over time. The same item ordered by different departments codes differently across properties — inconsistency that compounds at month-end and creates extra review work for the AP team.

The Answer

AI That Learns How Your Team Codes — by Role, by Property.

Reeco's AI learns from every invoice your team processes. A housekeeping manager coding nitrile gloves applies a different GL than a food & beverage manager ordering the same item. After a few coding cycles, the system handles the distinction automatically — no manual intervention required.

03
The Problem

No Single View Across All Properties for the AP Team.

The AP team currently has to access each property separately to review invoice status. There's no consolidated corporate view showing all 30 properties, what's pending approval, and what has been pushed to M3.

The Answer

One Login. All Properties. Configurable Permissions.

Reeco's corporate dashboard gives the AP team visibility across the entire portfolio from a single login. Approval workflows are fully configurable — GM codes, regional director approval gate for budget overages, AP review, then push to M3 — built to match how Lexima already operates, not the other way around.

Section 07  /  FAQs

Questions We Expect — Answered Directly.

If something's missing, email Zander and we'll add it before the follow-up call.

"We're still under contract with our current provider. How does the transition work?"
We understand you can't cut over overnight. Once you have clarity on your EPRO/Reactor Net contract end date, reach out and we'll work through business terms — including options to ease any overlap period. The goal is a clean switch, not a situation where you're paying for both systems longer than necessary.
"How fast can we actually be live?"
Reeco's AP automation is live within 1 week of contract execution. This is the fastest module we deploy — and the M3 preferred partnership is why. Your GL codes and vendor data are already in M3. We don't start from scratch. On Day 1, vendor email inboxes are live, invoices start flowing, and the M3 sync is active. By the end of Week 1, your AP team is processing invoices in Reeco and your failure queue is gone.
"Will the M3 integration actually work without the issues we have today?"
Yes. Reeco is M3's official Preferred Partner — this is not a third-party integration bolted on. The connection is direct. GL accounts pull from M3 automatically at setup. Invoices approved in Reeco sync to M3 automatically — posted and viewable from inside M3, not just from Reeco. No integration fees from Reeco or M3.
"What about adoption at the property level? Our GMs already have a lot on their plates."
Reeco is designed for the person who isn't a procurement professional. Invoices arrive by email, get coded automatically, and route for approval — GMs and department heads interact with a simple approval flow, not a complex system. The learning curve is minimal. The corporate dashboard means the AP team can manage everything centrally without logging into each property separately.
"What does your security and compliance posture look like?"
Reeco is SOC 2 Type II certified. Compliance documentation is available upon request — email zanders@reeco.com and we'll send the report within 24 hours. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Enterprise-grade cloud infrastructure with role-based access controls. Master Services Agreement available for legal review; multi-year terms available.
