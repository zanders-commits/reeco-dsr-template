section 02  /  business case + proposed solution

The problem you're solving — and how Reeco closes it.

Three pain points. Three direct answers.

01
the problem

Invoices failing to reach M3 — weekly, without warning.

Lexima's current system generates incorrect coding, blocked invoices, and duplicates on a weekly basis. When invoices don't flow through to M3, the failure queue has to be manually reviewed — a process that has become a standing part of the team's weekly routine. There's no proactive flagging; the error only surfaces during reconciliation.

the answer

Invoices sync to M3 automatically — no failure queue.

Reeco is M3's official Preferred Partner — the integration was built with M3, not around it. GL accounts and vendors sync directly from your M3 instance. Approved invoices flow back automatically, fully coded, with no manual exports and no month-end catch-up. Invoices are visible and viewable from M3 — not just from Reeco.

02
the problem

Manual coding that creates inconsistency at scale.

With the current system, GMs code invoices individually with no intelligence that learns over time. The same item ordered by different departments codes differently across properties — inconsistency that compounds at month-end and creates extra review work for the AP team.

the answer

AI that learns how your team codes — by role, by property.

Reeco's AI learns from every invoice your team processes. A housekeeping manager coding nitrile gloves applies a different GL than a food & beverage manager ordering the same item. After a few coding cycles, the system handles the distinction automatically — no manual intervention required.

03
the problem

No single view across all properties for the AP team.

The AP team currently has to access each property separately to review invoice status. There's no consolidated corporate view showing all 30 properties, what's pending approval, and what has been pushed to M3.

the answer

One login. All properties. Configurable permissions.

Reeco's corporate dashboard gives the AP team visibility across the entire portfolio from a single login. Approval workflows are fully configurable — GM codes, regional director approval gate for budget overages, AP review, then push to M3 — built to match how Lexima already operates, not the other way around.
